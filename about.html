About Sunshine Colours

Built to communicate a technically strong, premium, globally positioned frit manufacturing company with strong customization capability and broad industry coverage.

Who we are

High-performance frit solutions for modern industrial applications

Sunshine Colours is presented as a leading manufacturer and innovator in enamel frits, ceramic frits, abrasive frits, and glass colours. The company profile emphasizes technical strength, consistent quality, customization, and reliability, with operations positioned since 1998.

Its portfolio spans cookware enamels, automotive components, industrial equipment, architecture, glass decoration, abrasives, and specialty coatings.

Mission

To deliver reliable, performance-driven frit solutions, innovate around customer-specific needs, and enhance the durability and efficiency of clients’ end products.

Vision

To be a globally recognized leader in frit and coating solutions known for innovation, quality, customization, and customer satisfaction.

Why clients choose Sunshine Colours

  • 25+ years of experience
  • Strong technical and R&D focus
  • Customized development as per customer specifications
  • Wide product coverage across enamel, ceramic, abrasive and glass systems
  • Modern manufacturing and quality-controlled production approach
