Industry reach

Applications

Sunshine Colours products support demanding applications across enamel-coated metals, advanced ceramics, abrasives, diamond tools, and decorative glass industries.

Cookware & Kitchenware

Ground coats, cover coats, and decorative enamels for pots, pans, utensils, and food-contact metalware.

  • Ground coat systems
  • Transparent and colored cover coats
  • Stoneware and cookware glaze frits

Appliances & Industrial Enamel

Frit systems suited for appliance housings, panels, water heater tanks, industrial equipment, and durable enamel surfaces.

  • Appliance panels
  • Industrial tanks
  • Heat-resistant enamel components

Automotive & Exhaust Components

Enamel solutions for metal components requiring durability, corrosion resistance, and thermal stability in service conditions.

  • Silencers and exhaust systems
  • Automotive metal parts
  • Functional industrial coatings

Architecture & Signage

Glass and enamel systems for sign boards, panels, facade elements, and decorative architectural surfaces.

  • Sign boards
  • Architectural glass
  • Decorative panels

Abrasives & Diamond Tools

Bonding frits for vitrified wheels, cutting discs, grinding tools, core bits, and high-performance diamond segments.

  • Grinding wheels
  • Abrasive segments
  • Diamond cutting tools

Ceramics, Bone China & Porcelain

Premium transparent, opaque, and specialty frits for glaze development, surface finish, and decorative ceramic performance.

  • Stoneware
  • Bone china
  • Porcelain tableware

Decorative Glass & Decals

Lead-based and lead-free low-temperature frits for glass colours, decals, overglaze decoration, and specialty decorative systems.

  • Perfume bottles
  • Toughened glass decoration
  • Glass printing

Refractory & Industrial Linings

Crystalline transparent glaze frits for refractory products, protective coatings, and ceramic linings under thermal cycling.

  • Crucibles
  • Refractory glaze coatings
  • Protective ceramic linings

Custom Engineered Solutions

Collaborative formulation development for application-specific performance, tuning cost and technical outcomes together.

  • Customer-specific frit design
  • Import substitution
  • Performance optimization
